How to Use This Site

Upset by traditional popularity-based genre playlists? Classic Taste provides a litany of great pre-made playlists in different varieties of "classical" music using song network clustering techniques. Feel free to browse these playlists, play 30 seconds samples, or if you have a Spotify Account open your Spotify App and use this site as a remote!
